                
                  HOW TO PLAY

    - <LEFT> and <RIGHT> to choose position
    - <OK> or <DOWN> to place the coin

    The first player to align 4 coins of 
      their color wins!

    If you are up for a challenge, try not 
    to lose against the AI... even the 
    weakest one will not be so easy to beat!                              
    WARNING : the strong AI is so smart it
    takes pretty long to think... but I'm
    almost certain you can't beat it!
