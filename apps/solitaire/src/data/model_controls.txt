
                HOW TO PLAY

    - <ARROW KEYS> to move the cursor
    - <OK> to select/unselect current card
    - <SHIFT> and <ALPHA> to select more or
        less card of the current column
    - <BACK> to unselect
    - <SHIFT> to pause
    - <EXE> to use the deck
    - <TOOLBOX> to auto-place on foundations
    Search "solitaire rules" if you've never
    played any Solitaire. 
    Changing the difficulty changes 
    the number of cards displayed by each 
    turn of the deck.
